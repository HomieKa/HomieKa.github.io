TUCO Design Survey Analysis

Key Findings Summary

Color Palette Analysis

  • Nature Inspired was most preferred (59.1% adjusted preference)
  • 8 first-place rankings, 5 second-place rankings
  • Best average ranking position of 2.14
  • Combined Professional Nature showed strong performance (46.7%) despite late entry

Layout Preferences

  • Design 7 was most popular (27.3% of votes)
  • Designs 1, 2, and 4 tied for second (18.2% each)
  • Strong preference for clean, modern layouts
  • Consistent support for designs with clear navigation

Key Recommendations

  • Implement nature-inspired color scheme with professional elements
  • Focus on clean, modern design principles
  • Include prominent tracking features
  • Emphasize mission statement in design
  • Add easily accessible sales rep contact functionality
